Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 27, 2026 | Last Updated: March 27, 2026

ClearedPath ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of cleared professionals, military service members, and all users of our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide our services:

  • Email address — when you join our waitlist or create an account
  • Name — if you choose to provide it during signup
  • LinkedIn profile data — only if you explicitly connect your LinkedIn account, and only the data LinkedIn's API makes available (name, headline, connections at companies). We never access your messages or post on your behalf.
  • Resume content — if you use our Resume Translator or EPA/BRAG Sheet Analyzer, the text you paste is processed in your browser and is not stored on our servers.
  • Usage data — anonymous analytics such as pages visited, features used, and browser type. No personally identifiable information is included.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect security clearance documentation, SSNs, or government credentials
  • We do not store classified or sensitive compartmented information
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties
  • We do not share your data with recruiters or employers without your explicit consent

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage your waitlist position and notify you when access is available
  • To send product updates, feature announcements, and relevant content (you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • To improve our platform based on aggregate, anonymized usage patterns
  • To provide personalized job recommendations and career tools when the full platform launches

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is protected using industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption in transit — all data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
  • Encryption at rest — stored data is encrypted using AES-256
  • Access controls — only authorized personnel can access user data
  • No plaintext passwords — passwords are hashed using bcrypt
  • Regular security audits — we review our security posture regularly

Waitlist emails are stored with our third-party email service provider, which maintains SOC 2 compliance and encrypts all data at rest.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Waitlist management — to collect and manage waitlist signups
  • Analytics — anonymous usage analytics to improve the platform
  • LinkedIn OAuth — for optional LinkedIn account connection (when available)

Each third-party service has their own privacy policy. We only work with providers that meet our security standards.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correct your data — update or fix any inaccurate information
  • Delete your data — request complete removal of your information from our systems
  • Unsubscribe — opt out of marketing emails at any time via the unsubscribe link in every email
  • Export your data — receive your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@clearedpath.io.

7. Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Essential cookies — to remember your theme preference (dark/light mode) and login session
  • Analytics cookies — anonymous, aggregated data about how the site is used

We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels from ad networks.

8. Data Retention

We retain your email address for as long as you remain on our waitlist or maintain an account. If you request deletion, we remove your data within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated data (which cannot identify you) may be retained for product improvement.

9. Children's Privacy

ClearedPath is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via email to registered users. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled:

  • Email: privacy@clearedpath.io
  • Website: clearedpath.io
